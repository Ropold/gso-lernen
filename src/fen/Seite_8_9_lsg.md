#### Briefing

Orders are usually confirmed very quickly. Many companies that traditionally receive orders by post or fax have moved away from confirming orders and instead send their invoice directly in writing. This method represents a simplification; nevertheless, at least a check should be made that the order has been received. Companies that traditionally receive orders by post or fax use order confirmations to draw attention to further offers. Thus, they also provide new customers with trust and security. Even for large orders from regular customers, an order confirmation offers a certain degree of security and opens up opportunities for new orders.
